amgutowski.com and United States Copyright Laws


Ick, I have to talk aboutr legal stuff.

Hi, my name is Alex and I run this website. Any videos I post on YouTube or Vimeo will have copyright information in the description. It looks like this:

Copyright © 2020 Alex Gutowski
This video may be used with permission. Please visit amgutowski.com/copyright.html for more information.

Now, most people don't consider me a "party pooper" or the "fun governer", but laws are laws, and I have to respect them. After reading Title 17, Circular 92 of the United States Code, this is how I must proceed.

  • All videos will have a copyright statement in the description
  • All copyright statements will state one of four of the following
    1. This video may be used with permission.
    2. This video may not be used except by the publisher and those who appear in the copyright listing.
    3. This video may not by used by anyone except for the publisher.
    4. This video may be used without permission, giving credit in the form of "Copyright © {year} Alex Gutowski".
  • If the video has statements 1 or 2 in the description, you must fill out the form below to request a copyright release
  • If the video has statement 3 in the description, contact me directly.
  • If the video has statement 4 in the description, you may use the video, IF you give credit, either by placing this overlay in the bottom left corner of the video, or by writing Copyright © {year} Alex Gutowski. To get a copyright symbol in HTML, write "&copy;". To get a copyright symbol on Windows, hold 'Alt' and type 0169 on either your numberpad, or your keyboard. I'm not sure how to get one on Mac or Linux. Ask Google :). If you do not do this, I will copyright strike you.

Thank you for taking the time to read this. If you just skipped over it, that might not pay off. I will take these rules very seriously, and if I find any of my content online without my permission, I will copyright strike you, and request it to be taken down.
