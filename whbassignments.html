Holocaust Journal Entry


October 12, 1940
Dear Diary,
Today I woke up to find that the Nazis have trapped us in a small area that they are calling Ghettos. We have about 3 blocks for all the Jews in Poland, and the sidewalks are so crowded, people have to walk in the street! We now even have one way streets only for people, because the streets are so crowded! I hope Hitler changes his mind, because this sucks.



July 17, 1941
Dear Diary,
Hitler started killing Jews faster and faster. I can't believe I'm still alive... I just heard a loud noise from upstairs. I'm going to check what it is. Oh my gosh!! My parents are dead!! It's getting harder to breath. I don't know how much longer I will la...

(in case you couldn't tell, Hitler pumped Carbon Monoxide into their house.)
